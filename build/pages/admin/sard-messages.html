System Update 8 May 2017

Medical Role CSV Export (with job planning activities)

A new medical role CSV export has been added. It includes the PA breakdown of each user’s last complete job plan. This will allow administrators to see if Educational Supervisors and Clinical supervisors have allocated PAs for their supervision work in their job plan.

System Update 5 May 2017

All Appraisals CSV Export

The appraisals CSV export has been updated. It now includes the academic appraiser name and additional appraiser name.

System Update 4 May 2017

Medical Role CSV Export

The medical role CSV export has been updated. It now includes the last complete appraisal title, meeting date, completion date, appraiser name, academic appraiser name and additional appraiser name for each user in the medical group.

System Update 2 May 2017

eLeave Capacity Warnings

eLeave users can now view days when more than 25% of doctors will be on leave in their Speciality or Directorate.

System Update 6 April 2017

For trusts using appraisal month only:

We’ve made some changes to the Quarterly Report and AOA Report.

  1. These reports will now include dormant medics. If a medic is made dormant and has left the trust, please ensure their trust end date is set on their user account. (This will automatically ensure their prescribed connection end date is up to date and they are therefore excluded from future reports.)
  2. Medics that started at the trust after their appraisal month due date will no longer be excluded from the Quarterly Reports. In this situation, the appraisal carried out at their previous trust should be uploaded as an offline appraisal. For doctors who have recently completed training, it should be noted that their final ACRP equated to an appraisal in this context.

System Update 28 March 2017

MSF Action Needed page

We’ve added a page where administrators can see all the online MSFs for which an action needs to be taken, i.e. the doctor has completed colleague nomination or the doctor has marked their MSF as ready for analysis. It is possible to sort the MSFs by either of these options. The page can be found under MSF > Action Needed.

System Update 17 March 2017

Job Plan Templates

We’ve added a new feature to job planning to make it faster for doctors and new clients to get up and running with their job plans: Job Plan Templates!

Administrators can now create custom Job Plan Templates that users can select when starting a Job Plan. Users can choose from a Blank Template, Previous Job Plan and one of the Templates that you create. They can then proceed as normal with their job plan pre-filled with the data from the Template.

Templates are fully featured job plans, including Activities, so they can be set up just as a normal job plan. A few fields are disabled either because (in the case of effective date) that is calculated at the user side, or because it doesn’t make sense for them to be set centrally (e.g. Signatories, Meeting Info). To create a Template, go to Job Plans, Templates, New Job Plan Template. You can give the template a title, eg. Oncology Rota, and then click Save. As always please let us know your feedback and improvement requests!

eLeave CSV Export

Administrators can now export a CSV containing all leave on the system, via eLeave > eLeave Calendar > Export All Leave to CSV.

System Update 26 February 2017

Automatic update of prescribed connection start date and end date

If a medic has a prescribed connection to your trust’s designated body and their trust start date or trust end date is updated, the prescribed connection start and end date will now be automatically be changed to the same dates.

Update to Quarterly Report (appraisal month only)

The Quarterly Report has been updated for trusts using appraisal month. The ‘number of doctors with whom the designated body has a prescribed connection’ no longer excludes medics that don’t have an appraisal month set and no longer excludes medics that started after their appraisal month due date.

System Update 15 February 2017

Update to Job Plan Reports

We have updated all job planning report pages to exclude medics that do not have the job planning product and to exclude all medics that are currently exempt from job planning.

Update to Admin, All Users

We have optimised the Admin, ‘All Users’ page so it should be a lot faster now.
It now excludes dormant and dummy users by default, but they will be included in the search results.
There are separate pages to list medics and non medics.
The link to email all appraisers has been removed but it is available on the admin, Appraisals, Appraiser Management page.

System Update 21 November 2016

Job Plan Clash Checker

Work has been completed on adding functionality to check for clashes between job plan activities. You can view this functionality in action on the demo site at demo.sardjv.co.uk – if you would like this functionality to be activated at your trust just get in touch.

System Update 22 August 2016

Job Plan UI Improvements

The Timetable section of Job Planning has undergone a facelift – it is now clearer and easier to use, with separate tabs for the Overview and Weekly Calendar, new activities entered in a pop-up, and clearer labelling of form elements.

System Update 6 July 2016

Job Plan Subcategorisation Change

Admin – Job Plan – Subcategorisation is now called Admin – Job Plan – Activity Categorisation. If subcategorisation of job plan activities is turned on for your trust, we now use the terms Category, Subcategory and Activity rather than Category, Benchmark and Activity.

System Update 24 June 2016

Job Plan Signatory Behaviour Change

Job Plan signatures will no longer be revoked when changes are made to the signatories of a ‘Ready For Sign Off’ Job Plan. All (remaining) signatories will still be notified of the changes. This will reduce the need to resign job plans unnecessarily.

System Update 9 June 2016

Portfolio PDF Contents Page

We have done some work on the Portfolio PDF Contents page, so it is no longer spread over several pages by domain attributes but instead is a simpler, clearer list of the contents of the PDF. Let us know if this works for you and your team.

System Update 4 April 2016

All Users CSV Export

We have optimised the code that generates the CSV Export from Users/All Users – it is now approx 20x faster and will generate in under 20 seconds even at trusts with 1000+ users.

System Update 22 March 2016

Compliance change

We have made a change to appraisal compliance reporting for new starters:
If a doctor does not have an appraisal (online or offline), their compliance is marked as ‘unknown’. If the doctor has a ‘trust start date’ filled in, and this date is more than 12 months in the past, the compliance will change to ‘non-compliant’. If there is no ‘trust start date’ filled in, the compliance will remain ‘unknown’ until the doctor completes an appraisal.
